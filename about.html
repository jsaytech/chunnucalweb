HISTORY OF CHUNNACAL INVESTMENT LIMITED

Chunnacal Investment Ltd (otherwise called Chunnacal) was established in 2011 in Nigeria as a private company with a focus on various business activities. From inception, the company was driven by a commitment to providing high-quality products and services.

Early Years

During its early years, Chunnacal primarily focused on traditional retail operations. The company carved a niche for itself by fostering trust with customers/clients through transparent and ethical business practices.

Diversification

As years go by, the company started exploring opportunities beyond traditional retail operations.

Transition to E-commerce

As the world increasingly moved towards digital commerce, Chunnacal recognized the need to adapt. In 2023, the company made a strategic decision to launch its own online store, entering the booming e-commerce industry in Nigeria. This marked a significant shift in its business model from traditional retail operations to a tech-driven customer-focused online platform.

The online store aims to provide a wide range of products from Medical Equipment and Supplies to general goods (consumer goods, household items, printer consumables, apparels, etc). With its deep root in retail, the company is well positioned to leverage its experience and industry connections to build a successful online business.

Looking Ahead

Chunnacal is now focused on becoming a major player in Nigeria's online retail space, aiming to offer competitive pricing, convenience, and a superior customer experience. With this move, the company is embracing the future of retail, aligning itself with global trends in e-commerce and digitalization of business operations. The company remains dedicated to innovation, growth, and maintaining its legacy of trust, quality products and services as it ventures into digital economy.
